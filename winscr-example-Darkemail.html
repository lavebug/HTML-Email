Windscribe Policy Change

We have changed our policies. Watch the following video for details on these changes, and what they mean for you.

Watch Keynote Speech

Dear Windscribe Users,

Over the years, many of you have become familiar with Windscribe's humorous tone. This humour, alongside the quality of our software, has been responsible for garnering over 70,000,000 users. We have thoroughly enjoyed the ability to make you laugh, while providing you with the highest quality VPN software on the market. Recently, we have decided that Windscribe will be taking a more neutral approach with regards to our brand, less focused on humour, and more focused on maturing ourselves to reflect the professionalism of our company.

We have been in business for almost 8 wonderful years, growing from a tiny startup to a full-fledged privacy and security software company. Time does fly, and we are thankful to all of our users who have faithfully stuck by us on our journey so far. We are excited to usher in a more refined era, both internally with our staff, and externally with you and future customers. From this point forward, none of our users will have to worry about receiving offensive marketing material from us, and new users will no longer have to deal with the shock of unprofessional language inundating their inboxes.

We know that many of you will be upset at the change in tone, so we have hidden three promo codes in the video to ease you into the new Windscribe era. We understand that while these changes may seem drastic at first, you will all grow to appreciate them.

Fond regards,

Team Windscribe
